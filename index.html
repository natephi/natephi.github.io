Vision


It's 2031 and the digital world is as real as the living one.


A complete merging has taken place. People visit websites like they are visiting real places. People carry out business and covet rare digital assets. People transact everything and really everything under the Sun as fluidly as flowing water.


The genie is out of the bottle with blockchain technology. I firmly believe in this future. And I firmly believe that the value of a blockchain will be determined by its use-case.


Use-case investing, a framework that flirts with my mind, a framework that yet does not exist but will exist similar to the traditional value investing model.


I dedicate my mind to develop such a framework, to cut through the noise in crypto investing and discover the fundamental forces behind the market.
